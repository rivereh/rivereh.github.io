CourseXpo

CourseXpo is a collaborative virtual learning environment

  • Built in the Unity3D engine, users can connect into a 3d world to learn together.
  • Teachers can load educational content such as slides decks, quizzes, and videos.
  • Students can be grouped together with teacher defined groups to facilitate group learning.
  • Students can communicate with each other through voice or text chat.
  • The program is accessible through PC or can be sideloaded onto a Meta Quest.

Tower of Trials

Tower of Trials Short Game

  • Built in the Unity3D engine in 10 weeks with a group of students
  • Players are tasked with defeating enemies and reaching the top of the tower
  • Players can upgrade their armor and weapons on the ground floor of the tower
  • There are four different types of enemies with different stats to defeat
  • Animations are included for the player and ememies as well as visual armor upgrades
